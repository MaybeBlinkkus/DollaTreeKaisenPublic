The Dolla Tree Kaisen mod was originally a texture pack for vanilla Terraria authored by Nevilsico Shard which changed the looks of armor and accessories to look like JJK related items. However, given their permission, I (blinkkus) have turned it into a fully functioning mod.
This mod adds:
- 14 Vanity Armor Sets
- 5 Vanity Accessories

You may find the original texture pack here by Nevilisco Shard here: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3286355648
